Hank

Type Personal Project
Year 2026
Platform Web App / PWA
Stack Next.js · Claude AI
Overview

Hank is a home buying co-pilot built for UK first-time buyers. It walks users through every stage of the buying process — from assessing finances and getting a mortgage in principle, to making an offer and completing. The AI assistant, Hank, is powered by Claude (Anthropic) and provides personalised guidance throughout.

Live App
Features
  • 8-step interactive UK home buying roadmap with expandable steps, checklists, and video cards
  • AI assistant (Hank) powered by Claude — answers questions at any stage of the journey
  • Property tab with search, saved reviews, and offer tracking
  • Financial dashboard with deposit progress, LISA tracker, and live mortgage rate comparison
  • Paste in a Rightmove or Zoopla listing URL for instant AI property analysis
  • Onboarding flow capturing motivation, location, finances, and money mindset
  • Mobile-first PWA — 390px phone shell, feels native in browser

NFT Marketplace - Momint

Role Product Lead
Company Momint
Year 2022–2023
Platform Mobile App
The Challenge

Momint was Africa's first NFT marketplace. The MVP had launched, users were signing up - but the product was too complex for mainstream adoption. Web2 users didn't understand NFTs, the interface was cluttered, and conversion was low.

I joined in 2022 as Product Lead to simplify the product and drive growth.

My Role

I owned the full product lifecycle - strategy, roadmap, prioritisation, and delivery - working across a team of engineers, designers, and marketers.

The brief was clear: reduce friction, increase sales, and make blockchain accessible to everyday users without dumbing it down.

What I Focused On
  • Redesigning the discovery and purchase flow to reduce drop-off for first-time buyers
  • Simplifying the wallet and transaction experience for Web2-native users
  • Introducing ERC1155 smart contract functionality allowing sellers to edit multiple NFT prices simultaneously
  • Improving transparency and trust signals throughout the buying journey
Outcome

Scaled the app from MVP to 50K MAUs in 6 months. Increased new user signup completion rate by 60%. Improved month-3 retention by 25% through wallet redesign.

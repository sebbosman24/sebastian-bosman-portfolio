Vibe Coding — IOnlyListenToVinyl

Company Personal Project
Year 2025
Platform Web App

It's definitely ironic that I work in music tech and have recently felt the urge to experience human curation.

As a visual person, my favourite place to experience this is YouTube and taking it a step further, vinyl only DJ sets.

I started to notice others also appreciate human curation, so I built I Only Listen To Vinyl - a curated portal of vinyl only DJ sets.
